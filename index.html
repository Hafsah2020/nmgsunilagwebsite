About Us

The NMGS UNILAG (Nigerian Mining and Geosciences Society, University of Lagos Students' Chapter) exists to support and empower Geology and Geophysics students at the University of Lagos. Mission: Our mission is to foster academic excellence, build a strong sense of community, and prepare our members for successful careers in the Geosciences field.

Are you passionate about Geology and Geophysics? Do you want to excel in your studies, connect with like-minded peers, and gain valuable skills to prepare for a thriving career? Then NMGS UNILAG is the place for you! We offer a supportive environment with organized tutorials, engaging social activities, and industry insights from experienced professionals. Join us and unlock your full potential as a future Geoscientist! .
